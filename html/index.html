DEVICES:

- Lists connected devices based on MIDI connection
- Once connected, the device name, manufacturer, version, and ID will display in green text

COLORS:

COLOR SCALE: List of colors and their corresponding note
COLOR SCALE PRESETS: List of color scales from the past 300 years based on historical figures and
their own ideas on how the musical scale relates to color
TRANSPOSE COLORS: Moves each color up or down one pitch in the chromatic scale
RANDOMIZE: Randomizes all colors in the color scale

SETTINGS:

INNER RADIUS SIZE: How large the radius of the inner circle is drawn (1-999)
OUTER RADIUS SIZE: How large the radius of the outer circle is drawn (2-1000)
STARTING OPACITY: The starting transparency of the radial. (10% - 100%)
VELOCITY RATIO: How much the velocity of each note played effects the size of the radials (1:0 - 1:4)
NOTE RANGE: Adjusts what notes are displayed on the screen (C-1 - G#9)
FADE TIME: The time each note takes to fade out (No Fade / 0.1 sec - 10 sec)
DRAWING PATTERN: The pattern in which the radials are drawn
